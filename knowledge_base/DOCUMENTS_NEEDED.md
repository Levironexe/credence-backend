## Core Lending Regulations (Must-Have)

### 1. Law 32/2024/QH15 — Law on Credit Institutions 2024
- **Issued:** January 18, 2024 | **Effective:** July 1, 2024
- **Covers:** Lending limits (14% single borrower, phasing to 10% by 2029), credit extension, NPL management, disclosure requirements, organizational structure, bancassurance prohibition, bank run provisions, early intervention framework
- **Where to find:**
  - https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/EN/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Law-32-2024-QH15-Credit-institutions/606780/tieng-anh.aspx
  - https://english.luatvietnam.vn/tai-chinh/law-on-credit-institution-no-32-2024-qh15-296639-d1.html
  - https://vbma.org.vn/storage/legal-documents/October2024/Law%20of%20credit%20institutions.pdf

### 2. Circular 39/2016/TT-NHNN — Lending Transactions
- **Issued:** December 30, 2016 | **Effective:** March 15, 2017
- **Covers:** Borrower conditions (legal capacity, financial repayment capability), prohibited loan purposes, interest rate framework (market-driven + SBV caps), loan types (short/medium/long-term), 9 lending methods, loan agreement requirements, debt rescheduling, overdue debt conversion
- **Where to find:**
  - https://english.luatvietnam.vn/circular-no-39-2016-tt-nhnn-dated-december-30-2016-of-the-state-bank-of-vietnam-prescribing-lending-transactions-of-credit-institutions-and-or-forei-112177-doc1.html
  - https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/EN/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Circular-39-2016-TT-NHNN-lending-transactions-credit-institutions-foreign-bank-branches-with-customers/340380/tieng-anh.aspx

### 3. Circular 06/2023/TT-NHNN — Amendment to Circular 39
- **Issued:** June 28, 2023
- **Covers:** 4 new prohibited lending purposes (including lending for deposits), digital lending framework (level-3+ information systems required), updated interest rate provisions for transparent/healthy borrowers, repayment order (overdue principal first), financial reimbursement restrictions
- **Where to find:**
  - https://english.luatvietnam.vn/tai-chinh/circular-06-2023-tt-nhnn-provision-of-loans-by-credit-institutions-foreign-bank-branches-to-clients-257364-d1.html
  - https://lawnet.vn/en/vb/Circular-06-2023-TT-NHNN-amendments-Circular-39-2016-TT-NHNN-8B844.html

### 4. Circular 12/2024/TT-NHNN — Further Amendment to Circular 39
- **Issued:** June 28, 2024
- **Covers:** Latest amendments to lending transaction regulations
- **Where to find:**
  - https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/EN/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Circular-12-2024-TT-NHNN-amendments-to-Circular-39-2016-TT-NHNN-lending-transactions-of-credit-institutions/619929/tieng-anh.aspx

### 5. Circular 11/2021/TT-NHNN — Asset Classification & Risk Provisioning
- **Issued:** July 30, 2021 | **Effective:** October 1, 2021
- **Covers:** 5-group loan classification (Pass/Special Mention/Sub-standard/Doubtful/Potential Loss), NPL definition (Groups 3-5), provisioning rates (Group 1: 0%, Group 2: 5%, Group 3: 20%, Group 4: 50%, Group 5: 100%), general provision 0.75%, monthly self-classification requirement, CIC reporting
- **Where to find:**
  - https://english.luatvietnam.vn/circular-no-11-2021-tt-nhnn-dated-july-30-2021-of-the-state-bank-of-vietnam-providing-the-classification-of-assets-risk-provisioning-levels-and-met-206806-doc1.html
  - https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/EN/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Circular-11-2021-TT-NHNN-management-of-risks-arising-from-operations-of-credit-institutions/483968/tieng-anh.aspx

### 6. Circular 31/2024/TT-NHNN — Asset Classification (Replaces Circular 11)
- **Issued:** 2024
- **Covers:** Updated loan classification rules, Internal Credit Rating System (ICRS) requirements (Article 5: financial + non-financial indicators, qualitative + quantitative evaluation), must be applied uniformly across institution
- **Where to find:**
  - https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/EN/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Circular-31-2024-TT-NHNN/ (search on the site)

## Capital Adequacy — Basel II/III (Must-Have)

### 7. Circular 41/2016/TT-NHNN — Capital Adequacy Ratio (Basel II)
- **Issued:** December 30, 2016 | **Effective:** January 1, 2020
- **Covers:** Minimum CAR 8%, credit risk + operational risk + market risk calculation, semiannual reporting requirement, risk-weighted asset methodology under Basel II standardized approach
- **Where to find:**
  - https://english.luatvietnam.vn/ (search "Circular 41/2016/TT-NHNN")
  - https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/EN/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Circular-41-2016-TT-NHNN/ (search on the site)

### 8. Circular 14/2025/TT-NHNN — Capital Adequacy Ratio (Basel III)
- **Issued:** June 30, 2025 (replaces Circular 41/2016)
- **Covers:** CET1 ratio 4.5%, Tier 1 ratio 6%, total CAR 8%, capital conservation buffer (CCB) 2.5%, counter-cyclical buffer (CCyB), Tier 1/Tier 2 capital quality standards, tiered risk weights (20%-150% based on asset type/collateral/LTV), IRB approach available from September 2025, mandatory standardized approach from January 2030
- **Where to find:**
  - https://thuvienphapluat.vn/ (search "Circular 14/2025/TT-NHNN")
  - https://english.luatvietnam.vn/ (search "Circular 14/2025")

## Anti-Money Laundering (Must-Have)

### 13. Law 14/2022/QH15 — Anti-Money Laundering
- **Issued:** November 15, 2022 | **Effective:** March 1, 2023
- **Covers:** Customer due diligence (CDD) requirements, KYC for 5 customer subgroups, reporting threshold VND 400 million, suspicious transaction reporting (9 signs), risk-based customer classification (low/medium/high), record retention 5 years, PEP definitions (broadened to include international org leaders), correspondent banking rules, annual risk assessment requirement
- **Where to find:**
  - https://english.luatvietnam.vn/ (search "Law 14/2022/QH15")
  - https://thuvienphapluat.vn/ (search "Law 14/2022/QH15")

## Fintech & Innovation (Must-Have)

### 14. Decree 94/2025/ND-CP — Fintech Regulatory Sandbox
- **Issued:** April 29, 2025 | **Effective:** July 1, 2025
- **Covers:** 3 sandbox categories (credit scoring, Open API, P2P lending), credit institutions limited to credit scoring + Open API, fintech companies can do all 3, 2-year license (extendable twice, 1 year each), P2P requirements (Vietnamese-owned only, CIC data mandatory, bank account/e-wallet transactions only, max 2-year loan terms, no guarantees), leadership must have 2+ years finance/banking/tech experience, testing must start within 90 days
- **Where to find:**
  - https://thuvienphapluat.vn/ (search "Decree 94/2025/ND-CP")
  - https://english.luatvietnam.vn/ (search "Decree 94/2025")

## Instructions

1. Download English PDF versions of as many documents above as possible
2. Place all PDFs in this folder: `credence-backend/knowledge_base/`
3. Name files clearly, e.g.:
   - `Law_32_2024_QH15_Credit_Institutions.pdf`
   - `Circular_39_2016_TT_NHNN_Lending.pdf`
   - `Circular_11_2021_TT_NHNN_Asset_Classification.pdf`
   - `Law_14_2022_QH15_AML.pdf`
   - etc.
4. Run the ingestion script (will be created): `python scripts/ingest_lending_knowledge.py`

The script will:
- Extract text from all PDFs using pdfplumber
- Chunk text with RecursiveCharacterTextSplitter (512 tokens, 100 overlap)
- Embed with OpenAI text-embedding-3-small
- Store in pgvector for semantic search
- Tag each chunk with source document metadata (law number, category, jurisdiction)
