# Decree 94/2025/ND-CP — Fintech Regulatory Sandbox

**Issued:** April 29, 2025
**Effective:** July 1, 2025
**Jurisdiction:** Vietnam

Vietnam's fintech sandbox framework enabling controlled testing of innovative financial services.

## 3 Sandbox Categories

1. **Credit Scoring:** Use of alternative data and AI/ML for credit assessment
2. **Open API:** Sharing financial data between institutions via standardized APIs
3. **Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Lending:** Direct lending between individuals/businesses via platforms

## Who Can Participate

- **Credit institutions:** Limited to credit scoring and Open API categories
- **Fintech companies:** Can participate in all 3 categories
- **Joint ventures** between credit institutions and fintech companies

## Sandbox License

- Duration: 2 years (initial)
- Extensions: Up to 2 extensions, each for 1 year (maximum total: 4 years)
- Testing must start within 90 days of license issuance
- Quarterly reporting to SBV required

## P2P Lending Requirements

- **Ownership:** Must be Vietnamese-owned (≥51% domestic ownership)
- **CIC data:** MUST use CIC credit data in risk assessment
- **Transaction channels:** All transactions must go through bank accounts or e-wallets (no cash)
- **Maximum loan term:** 2 years
- **No guarantees:** P2P platforms must not guarantee repayment to lenders
- **Information disclosure:** Must clearly display risk warnings and historical default rates

## Leadership Requirements

- CEO and senior management must have at least 2 years of experience in finance, banking, or technology
- Must have a Chief Risk Officer
- Must have an independent compliance officer

## Credit Scoring Innovation

- Companies can use alternative data sources: mobile data, transaction history, social data
- AI/ML models must be explainable — the SBV requires documentation of model methodology
- Models must be tested for bias across demographic groups
- Regular model validation (at least annually) is required
