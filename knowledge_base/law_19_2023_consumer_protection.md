# Law 19/2023/QH15 — Protection of Consumers' Rights

**Issued:** June 20, 2023
**Effective:** July 1, 2024
**Jurisdiction:** Vietnam

This law significantly strengthens consumer protection in financial services, including lending. It contains 7 chapters and 80 articles.

## Pre-Contract Disclosure Obligations

Before entering into a loan agreement, the credit institution MUST provide:

- Clear explanation of all terms and conditions
- Total cost of borrowing (effective interest rate, fees, charges)
- Repayment schedule with breakdown of principal and interest
- Consequences of default (penalty interest, collection procedures)
- Right to early repayment and any associated fees
- Cooling-off period (if applicable)

## Standard Form Contract Requirements

- **Minimum font size:** 12 point
- **Language:** Must be in Vietnamese (bilingual contracts allowed but Vietnamese version prevails)
- **Ambiguity rule:** If a clause can be interpreted in multiple ways, the interpretation favoring the consumer prevails
- **Prohibited clauses:** Clauses that unilaterally change terms, limit the institution's liability, or force arbitration without consumer consent

## Consumer Data Protection

- **Consent:** Must obtain explicit consent before collecting or using personal data
- **Purpose limitation:** Data can only be used for the stated purpose
- **Right to access:** Consumers can request a copy of their data
- **Right to correction:** Consumers can request correction of inaccurate data
- **Right to deletion:** Consumers can request deletion when data is no longer necessary

## Digital Platform Transparency

- Online lending platforms must clearly display: lender identity, interest rate range, fee structure
- Must provide a 5-day disclosure period before any modification to loan terms
- Must maintain customer service accessible via multiple channels

## Penalties

- Fines ranging from VND 10 million to VND 200 million per violation
- Repeated violations may result in license suspension
- Consumers have the right to file complaints with the competition authority
